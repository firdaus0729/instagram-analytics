# How to Convert Instagram Account to Business or Creator

The JustInfluence platform requires Instagram **Business** or **Creator** accounts (not personal accounts) to access analytics and insights.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Option 1: Convert to Business Account (Recommended)

1. **Open Instagram App** on your phone
2. **Go to your Profile** (tap your profile picture)
3. **Tap the Menu** (three horizontal lines) in the top right
4. **Tap Settings** (gear icon)
5. **Tap Account**
6. **Scroll down and tap "Switch to Professional Account"**
7. **Choose "Business"**
8. **Follow the prompts** to:
   - Connect to a Facebook Page (required for Business accounts)
   - Add business contact information
   - Complete your business profile

### Option 2: Convert to Creator Account

1. **Open Instagram App** on your phone
2. **Go to your Profile**
3. **Tap the Menu** (three horizontal lines)
4. **Tap Settings**
5. **Tap Account**
6. **Tap "Switch to Professional Account"**
7. **Choose "Creator"** (instead of Business)
8. **Follow the prompts** to set up your creator profile

## Troubleshooting

### "No Instagram Business Account Found"

**Problem:** Your Instagram account is connected to a Facebook Page, but the app can't find it.

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure your Instagram account is connected to a Facebook Page:
   - Instagram App â†’ Settings â†’ Account â†’ Linked Accounts â†’ Facebook
   - Ensure a Facebook Page is linked (not just a personal profile)

2. Check Facebook Page Settings:
   - Go to your Facebook Page
   - Settings â†’ Instagram
   - Make sure your Instagram account is connected

3. Verify Business Account Status:
   - Instagram App â†’ Settings â†’ Account
   - You should see "Professional Account" or "Business Account"

### "Account Not Eligible"

**Problem:** Your account might not meet Instagram's requirements.

**Solutions:**
- Ensure your account is at least a few days old
- Make sure you have some posts/content
- Check if your account is in good standing (not restricted)

### Still Having Issues?

1. **Log out and log back in** to Instagram
2. **Unlink and re-link** your Facebook Page
3. **Wait 24 hours** and try again (sometimes changes take time to propagate)
4. **Contact Support** if the issue persists
